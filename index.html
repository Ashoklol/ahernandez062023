About Me

Ashley Hernandez is a high school student who grew up in the small town of Huron in the County of Fresno. She can do lots of different styles of editing (videos) and a lot of photoshop. She can also produce different types and genres of music styles from sad, all the way to hard rock and/or metal. She loves to listen to music, has years of experience, and is really professional at mixing and mastering. She is also able to play various instruments like the violin, recorder, and trombone. She can even code HTML and CSS plus, a bit of JavaScript. Her goal is to keep learning as she continues to produce music. She has expertise in using an online platform that can produce, manipulate, and edit music.
